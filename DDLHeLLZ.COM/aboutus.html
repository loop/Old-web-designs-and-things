About Us

We started off in March 2009, as a simple place for people to download roms.. mostly for me and my mates to share what we loved. It was just a simple download script on a single server, i knew some HTML and PHP and decided.. why dont we turn this into a site for the public, so we did.

At this point, i was using up 1GB day of bandwith alone for DDLHeLLZ, so we upgraded to a german server host... and we have been with them for a while and its great. We also decided that our motto would be - No advertising, No voting, Just Roms. And we stick by it. We started uploading 100 roms a day, leaving our FTPs on going whilst doing other things in reality, but after a while DDLHeLLZ took its place in the world by getting a link on Gameboy-Advance-Roms... and then the traffic starting pouring in.

Our "advertising" manager has somehow managed to get us alot of traffic, and i must say, it was to much. We werent ready for this sudden change and suddenly BOOM, we had to rent a rack of server after 24hours of what we originally thought to be a DDoS attack! The site grows more and more each day, and we are here to stay. I have actually been to the servers and tweaked them up a bit making them faster, its great to actually beable to see what makes your sites work.
