RedSpiral 3.0

RedSpiral 3.0 is here, lots of changes to come, new design, updates to scripts and also things that will make it easier for developers to collaborate and work together on the current and new scripts.

Update: Due to the new design not working perfectly across all browsers, the design is back to a simple one until we can sort out the design flaws. Also an advantage of this new simple design is the site load time, pretty much in milliseconds.
